### 🤝 Behavioural & Scenario-Based Interview Questions

##### Technical knowledge is only one part of becoming a successful AI Engineer. Employers also want to understand how you solve problems, learn new technologies, work with others, handle setbacks, and communicate technical ideas to different audiences.

##### This section focuses on behavioural and scenario-based interview questions that are commonly asked during AI Engineering, Data Engineering, Software Engineering, and Cloud Engineering interviews.

##### Remember that interviewers are not looking for perfect stories. They want to understand your thought process, how you respond to challenges, and how you continue learning.

---

### ❓Question 48

## Tell me about one of the biggest challenges you faced during this project.

##### 🎯 What the interviewer is testing

Problem-solving ability.

##### 💬 Professional Answer

> One of the biggest challenges was integrating multiple technologies that all had different responsibilities. Claude Desktop, FastMCP, Python, Databricks SQL Warehouse, REST APIs, and SQL queries all needed to communicate correctly. Rather than trying to solve everything at once, I broke the problem into smaller parts. I first ensured each component worked independently before integrating them step by step. This approach made debugging much easier and helped me better understand how each layer contributed to the overall architecture.

##### 👶 Explain Like I'm Five

##### Instead of trying to build the whole LEGO city at once,

I built one building at a time.

### ❓Question 49

## Tell me about a bug you encountered.

##### 🎯 What the interviewer is testing

Debugging skills.

##### 💬 Professional Answer

> During development, I encountered several issues such as incorrect file paths, SQL query failures, configuration errors, and Databricks authentication problems. Rather than guessing the solution, I used logging, error messages, and unit tests to isolate the root cause. This reinforced the importance of structured debugging rather than trial-and-error programming.

##### 🚀 Bonus Points

Mention:

- Reading stack traces
- Using logs
- Running unit tests

### ❓Question 50

## How do you approach debugging?

##### 🎯 What the interviewer is testing

Logical thinking.

##### 💬 Professional Answer

> I follow a structured debugging process. First, I reproduce the problem consistently. Next, I read the error message carefully, review the application logs, verify configuration values, isolate the affected component, and use unit tests to confirm the fix. Finally, I rerun the full test suite to ensure no existing functionality has been broken.

##### 👶 Explain Like I'm Five

##### I don't guess.

I investigate.

### 🌟 Final Interview Advice

##### Every interview is an opportunity to tell the story of your project.

##### Rather than listing technologies, explain:

- The business problem.
- Your design decisions.
- The challenges you overcame.
- The lessons you learned.
- How you would improve the project in the future.

##### Interviewers remember stories far more than lists of technologies.
